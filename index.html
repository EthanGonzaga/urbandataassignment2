About

Australia is considered one of the most multicultural society's in the world, with a majority of immigrants arriving from overseas making their first port of call in NSW, more specifically Sydney.

Sydney is a mix of different cultures and ethinicties with a majority of which tend to live in clusters that include a multitude of suburbs; with ethnic clusters begininng with the first immigrants of that certain ethnicity.

This website aims to analyse the G10 dataset retrieved from the Australian Bureau of Statistics which contains data on Country of Birth of Person by Year of Arrival in Australia (in NSW). More specifically looking into three countries of:
